Our Approach

Intelligence,
Not Listings.

The AI job market generates more noise than signal. Thousands of postings, most of them ghost jobs. Salary ranges so wide they're meaningless. LinkedIn influencers telling you to "learn prompt engineering" six months after the window closed.

TopAIJobs.com exists to cut through that. We apply intelligence-grade analysis to the AI employment landscape — the same methodology used in audience intelligence and market research — to surface what actually matters.

Which roles are real. Which companies are genuinely building. Which skills carry a premium. Which career moves have asymmetric upside.

We don't scrape and list. We research, rank, and explain.
